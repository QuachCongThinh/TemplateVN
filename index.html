VIET NAM

Vietnam, whose national name is the Socialist Republic of Vietnam, is a country located at the easternmost tip of the Indochinese peninsula in Southeast Asia, bordering Laos, Cambodia, China, the East Sea and the South China Sea. Gulf of Thailand.

Vietnam has an area of 331,698 kmÂ², located in the east of the Indochina peninsula, in Southeast Asia. Sharing a border with three countries, Vietnam borders China to the north, Laos and Cambodia to the west, and the East Sea to the east. In 2019, the population of Vietnam is more than 96 million people, ranking 13th in the world. Hanoi, the capital of Vietnam, is the second largest city with 6.2 million people, after Ho Chi Minh City, 8.8 million people.

Vietnam is a tropical country with mostly mountainous terrain (accounting for 3/4 of the territory), mainly low hills, and plains accounting for only 1/4 of the area. Across the country, plain terrain and low mountains (under 1,000 m) account for 85% of the area. High mountain terrain (over 2,000 m) accounts for only 1% of the country's area. The topographical structure is quite diverse thanks to the rejuvenation of the Neo-tectonic movement, creating a clear gradation by altitude, descending from the northwest to the southeast. Land that can be used for agriculture accounts for less than 20%. The country is divided into mountainous areas and the Red River Delta in the north; Truong Son range, Central Highlands, central coastal plain, and Mekong Delta in the south.

Vietnamese Heritage

- The whole country has more than 40,000 relics and landscapes, of which more than 3,000 relics are ranked national relics, 5,000 relics are ranked provincial level. The Red River Delta is the area with the highest density and number of relics, up to 70%. In addition, nationwide, Vietnam also has 117 museums - places that preserve the nation's heroic history.
- Vietnam is one of the few countries in the world that has so many heritage sites recognized by UNESCO. Up to now, our country has 8 heritage sites recognized by UNESCO: Thang Long Imperial Citadel, Hoi An ancient town, Trang An scenic complex, Hue ancient capital, Ho Dynasty citadel, My Son sanctuary, national park. Phong Nha - Ke Bang area, Ha Long Bay. This is one of Vietnam's tourism potentials to attract international visitors.

Scenic spots

- Vietnam has been recognized by UNESCO as 8 world biosphere reserves, including: Red River Delta, Cat Ba, Tay Nghe An, Dong Nai, Cu Lao Cham, Can Gio, Ca Mau and Kien Giang Sea. It can be said that our country has both "golden forests and silver seas". Not only is it strong in the potential for developing Vietnam's marine tourism, but the plains, mountains and midlands of our country also possess countless "memorable" landscapes for tourists.
- The potential for developing Vietnam's beach tourism is most clearly demonstrated in the summer - the time when the need for cool bathing increases. Meanwhile, autumn - winter and spring in mountainous areas such as the Northwest and Central Highlands attract diners because of the flower seasons and the romantic, poetic atmosphere. Some beautiful places to visit in the mountainous provinces in Vietnam: Moc Chau in the season of mustard flowers and plum blossoms, Da Lat with pink grass hills in early winter, the Northwest in ripe rice season, Gia Lai in the season of bright yellow cassia flowers...

Culture and Cuisine

- Culture and Cuisine are two of the tourism potentials that need to be preserved and developed. Vietnam has 54 ethnic groups, each region and each ethnic group has its own cultural identity, customs, and lifestyle, creating its own charm. Not only that, Vietnam also has famous intangible cultural heritage: Hue royal court music, Central Highlands gong cultural space, ca tru, quan ho, Xoan singing, Giong festival, Hung worshiping beliefs. Vuong (March 10 of the lunar calendar)... to attract tourists.
- Thanks to the diversity in national culture, the cuisine in our country is equally rich. Vietnam was honored to be in the Top 15 countries with the most attractive street food in the world. Some Vietnamese dishes are loved by international friends such as: Pho, banh mi, Hue beef noodle soup..
